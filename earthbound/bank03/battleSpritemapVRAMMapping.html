    Variable battleSpritemapVRAMMapping

    Mappings for each chunk of tile data associated with a 32x32 hardware sprite

    immutable(ushort[32]) battleSpritemapVRAMMapping = [cast(ushort)0u, cast(ushort)128u, cast(ushort)256u, cast(ushort)384u, cast(ushort)2048u, cast(ushort)2176u, cast(ushort)2304u, cast(ushort)2432u, cast(ushort)4096u, cast(ushort)4224u, cast(ushort)4352u, cast(ushort)4480u, cast(ushort)6144u, cast(ushort)6272u, cast(ushort)6400u, cast(ushort)6528u, cast(ushort)8192u, cast(ushort)8320u, cast(ushort)8448u, cast(ushort)8576u, cast(ushort)10240u, cast(ushort)10368u, cast(ushort)10496u, cast(ushort)10624u, cast(ushort)12288u, cast(ushort)12416u, cast(ushort)12544u, cast(ushort)12672u, cast(ushort)14336u, cast(ushort)14464u, cast(ushort)14592u, cast(ushort)14720u];

    This enables 32 chunks of 32x32 tile data to fit into a 0x4000 byte buffer without any gaps. Note that the offsets aren't evenly spaced - this is because the sprites are made up of 4x4 8x8 chunks of tile data laid out in two dimensions instead of linearly
